## Production notes
- **Music:** Low-key lo-fi instrumental, 90-100 BPM. No lyrics. No trending audio. (Sana's rule: trends age, brand compounds.)
- **Pacing:** Cuts every 1.5-2s during the lever sections to hold attention. Beat-on-beat with the music.
- **Captions:** Burned in, JetBrains Mono, signal-cyan or white. Never relying on platform auto-caption.
- **Voice:** Direct, lower register, no upspeak. Builder talking to builder.
- **Loop test:** Final frame must visually rhyme with frame 1 — black bg, big number, same type. Re-watch should feel intentional, not jarring.
